# Player Characters

## Cato

**Ancestry:** Human, male
**Class:** Champion of Asmodeus
**Weapon:** Flail + Shield
**Worships:** Asmodeus
**Focus:** Defence and punishing enemies for acting against him or his team. Bounty hunter flavour via joining The Order of the Chain.

### Key facts

- Won a contest during an Asmodean festival in Stormhaven, bound to a contract to pay off a powerful magical item. The item's greatest power is kept in check until the debt is paid, and it is cursed to be un-removable until then.
- Joined the Order of the Chain via 1-on-1 battle with a demon. Proper Hellknight.
- Secretly getting orders from the Asmodean high priest and inquisitor to infiltrate and destabilise the [Abadarian faction](factions/abadar.md).
- Secretly reporting on the team's actions and betraying secrets to the Asmodeans.
- Has a crush on the Asmodean inquisitor.

## Casseus The Gorgon

**Ancestry:** Minotaur
**Class:** Demonic Dragon Instinct Barbarian
**Weapon:** Dorn-Dergar
**Worships:** Nobody in particular, but appreciates Asmodean values of discipline.
**Focus:** "Commander" flavour via the Marshal Dedication (intimidation focused).

### Key facts

- Hired spies (beggars) via the team's butler, Sayon Goro. Can be used to feed the team rumours — things a beggar might hear on the street, no big secrets.
- Sayon Goro was secretly almost immediately recruited by the wizard [Constantine Lightspinner](npcs/constantine-lightspinner.md) to spy on the team.

## Vittoria

**Ancestry:** Elf
**Class:** Companion Inventor
**Weapon:** Short bow
**Worships:** [Pharasma](factions/pharasma.md)
**Focus:** Her companion Gary the robot (class primary feature), team healer via Medic Dedication and Ancient Elf giving Cleric of Pharasma Dedication. Wildly effective healing so long as there aren't too many fights per day. The Pharasman cleric angle has been a major campaign driver.

### Key facts

- Charged by [Lady So-Yun](npcs/so-yun-nightshade.md) to keep an eye on the team and prevent them causing too much political trouble in town. That's not going great.
- The team took over a nearby fortress (2–4 days march away), staffed it with poor people and criminal werewolf renegades on the run for nearly a decade.
- They're secretly funnelling building materials to the fort and have acquired a ship from the Abadarian inquisitor — formerly a [Free Sails](factions/free-sails.md) mobile drug lab operated by an alchemist from the House of Storms.
- This has pissed off the Free Sails, the House of Storms, and the Gozrans.
- They've also taken part in fracking in the hills around Stormhaven on behalf of the [Smokers](factions/smokers.md) and Carvers, which pissed off the druids.

## Belwar

**Ancestry:** Gnome
**Class:** Magus — Horizon Spellshell (Team+ subclass, uses bounded essence casting from Magic+)
**Weapon:** Crossbow (started as a gun, prefers the bigger damage dice; subclass helps action economy)
**Worships:** Cayden Cailean
**Focus:** Damage with a side order of magical knowledge and utility.

### Key facts

- Doesn't engage much socially. Attempted Free Sails hook didn't land with the player.

## Frollo

**Ancestry:** Human
**Class:** Warpriest Cleric of Irori (uses essence casting from Magic+)
**Weapon:** Fists / unarmed combat + shield
**Worships:** Irori
**Focus:** Front-line support, backup healer, locks down enemies via athletics and the Wrestler Dedication. Whirling Throw is a crowd favourite.

### Key facts

- No Irori representation in Stormhaven. Applying to take the position of High Priest of Irori.
- Started a yoga studio in Abadar's Bazaar — helping people towards their physical peak and offering priestly advice.
- Working with the [Abadarians](factions/abadar.md) on behalf of Cato, who is secretly getting orders from the Asmodean high priest and inquisitor to infiltrate and destabilise the Abadarian faction.
