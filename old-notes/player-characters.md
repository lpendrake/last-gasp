# Characters

## Player Characters

### Cato

Ancestry: Human, male  
Class: Champion of Asmodeus  
Weapon of choice:Flail \+ Shield  
Worships: Asmodenus  
Notes: Focused on defence and punishing enemies for daring to act against him or his team.  
Bounty hunter flavour via joining The Order of the Chain.

#### Remember

Won a contest during an asmodean festival in Stormhaven, but was bound to a contract to pay off a powerful magical item.  
The item’s greatest power is kept in check until he pays off the debt and it is cursed to be un-removable until the debt is payed.  
Joined the Order of the Chain via 1 on 1 battle with a demon. He’s a proper Hellknight.  
is secretly getting orders from the asmodean high priest and inquisitor to infiltrate and try to destabilise the abadarian faction.  
Is secretly reporting on the teams actions and betraying secrets to the asmodeans.  
Has a crush on the asmodean inquisitor.

### Casseus The Gorgon

Ancestry: Minotaur  
Class: Demonic Dragon Instinct Barbarian  
Weapon of choice: Dorn-Dergar  
Worships: Nobody in particular, but appreciates asmodean values of discipline.  
Notes: focused on a “commander” flavour via the Marshal Dedication (intimidation focused)

#### Remember

Sayon Goro was secretly almost immediately recruited by the wizard Lightspinner to spy on the team.

### Vittoria

Ancestry: Elf  
Class: Companion Inventor  
Weapon of choice: Short bow  
Worships: Pharasma  
Notes: Focused on her companion, Gary the robot, which is her class’ primary feature and being the team healer via the Medic Dedication and Ancient Elf giving her a Cleric of Pharasma Dedication. Wildly effective healing so long there’s not too many fights per day.   
The pharasman cleric angle has played a major role in the campaign so far.

#### Remember

Charged by Lady So-Yun to keep an eye on the team and keep them from causing too much trouble in town, politically speaking.  
That’s not going great. The team took over a nearby fortress (2-4 days march away), staffed it with poor people and criminal were-wolf renegades who’ve been on the run for near a decade and are secretly funneling building materials away to the fort but have now also acquired a ship from the Abadarian inquisitor that used to belong to Free Sails as a mobile drug lab which was operated by an alchemist from the house of storms.   
That’s managed to piss off the free sails, the house of storms, and gozrans.  
They’ve also taken part in fracking in the hills around stormhaven on behalf of the Smokers and Carvers, which pissed off the druids.
