# The Quarry

A [Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md) quarry in the hills around [Stormhaven](stormhaven.md). Site of the plane of Earth portal that opened on Gozran 28 when a tentacled creature tore through reality during a Smokers excavation. The earth elemental that appeared stabilised the portal at the cost of its own life.

[Gorbeka](../npcs/gorbeka.md) was brought here to take measurements for a lightning-rod solution. The portal was subsequently closed by the party (Desnus 4).
