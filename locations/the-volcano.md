# The Volcano

An active volcano on [Emberheart Island](emberheart-island.md). Houses a trapped elder creature of immense power — something from between the stars, so powerful the Ancients could only seal it away after an enormous effort. Its thrashing within its cage is destabilising the magical matrix that powers both the volcano containment and the island's barrier.

The concentration of its power turned the mountain into a volcano when it was first trapped.
