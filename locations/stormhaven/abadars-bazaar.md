# Abadar's Bazaar

The main commercial district of [Stormhaven](../stormhaven.md), under the oversight of the [Keystone Council (Abadar)](../../factions/abadar.md). Site of [Frollo](../../party.md)'s yoga studio / priestly advice practice. [Sim Silvershield](../../npcs/sim-silvershield.md) is frequently found gambling here.

A man combusted with blue electricity in this district on approximately Desnus 3, sparking the investigation into [Aella Stormcaller](../../npcs/aella-stormcaller.md)'s drug operation.
