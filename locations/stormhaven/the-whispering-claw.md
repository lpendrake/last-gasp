# The Whispering Claw

The occult witch tower of [The Three Towers](../../factions/three-towers.md) in [Stormhaven](../stormhaven.md). Home of [The Whispering Claw](../../factions/the-whispering-claw.md) witch faction, led by High Witch [Silas Fellswitch](../../npcs/silas-fellswitch.md). [Cassia](../../npcs/cassia.md) is an Adept here.
