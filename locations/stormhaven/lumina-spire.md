# The Lumina Spire

A tall magi-tech lighthouse in [Stormhaven](../stormhaven.md), built by the Ancients but renovated and inhabited by [Constantine Lightspinner](../../npcs/constantine-lightspinner.md). Home of [The Lumina Spire](../../factions/lumina-spire.md) arcane tower, one of [The Three Towers](../../factions/three-towers.md).

Located in the north-east docks area, near [The Shifting Sands](the-shifting-sands.md) inn.
