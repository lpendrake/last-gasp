# The Keep

The party's fortified base of operations, located approximately 2–4 days' march from [Stormhaven](stormhaven.md) (or accessible by sailboat in about 1.2gp per person via a cove near the keep). Staffed with 29 poor recruits, werewolf renegades, and led by [Mother Spoons](../npcs/elara-coppertress.md) as elected 2nd in command.

The party is covertly funnelling building materials here from Stormhaven. Upgrade system uses a point-based progress system with quest rewards.
