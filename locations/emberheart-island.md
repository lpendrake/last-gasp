# Emberheart Island

The island on which [Stormhaven](stormhaven.md) sits. Surrounded by an impenetrable magical barrier — a ring of rapidly swirling water reaching to the sea floor, above which winds defy any sailor's ability to cross. Teleportation magic fails dramatically when used to escape. The barrier is an ancient prison.

Home to hostile creatures, ancient ruins, volcanic activity, and multiple factions of Ancients. [The volcano](the-volcano.md) on the island houses a trapped elder creature of immense power.
