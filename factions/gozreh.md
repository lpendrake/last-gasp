# Tidecallers (Gozreh)

Worshipped out of desperation. Some believe he is holding back the wall of fog. [House of Storms](house-of-storms.md) is the primary benefactor — he's their patron deity.

**Slang:** Breezers.

### [Elara Stormweaver](../npcs/elara-stormweaver.md)
Technically a witch whose patron is Gozreh. Through her, and others in her family back on the mainland, he has some influence on civilisation.

### [Fiora Greenleaf](../npcs/fiora-greenleaf.md)
A passionate advocate for environmental conservation. She's often seen leading expeditions to the island's wilderness and is frequently found in the Free Sails' territory, given their connection to the sea and nature.

### [Kaelan Stormcaller](../npcs/kaelan-stormcaller.md)
A former pirate who found redemption in the worship of Gozreh. He's a skilled sailor and often leads the Free Sails in their fishing expeditions.
