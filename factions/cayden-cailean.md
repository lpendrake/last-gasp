# Cup Bearers (Cayden Cailean)

Refined alcohols, large boarding houses. Commission a number of crafters to create adventuring essentials. Primarily worshipped within the House of Storms due to their control of the drinking establishments.

**Slang:** Caydians, the drunks, the gamblers.

### [Kaelan Redfist](../npcs/kaelan-redfist.md)
A boisterous and jovial half-orc, known for his love of ale, games, and good company. He believes in living life to the fullest. He can often be found in the taverns of the House of Storms or the Free Sails' floating city.

### [Fiora Sunbright](../npcs/fiora-sunbright.md)
A talented musician and performer. She often organises festivals and celebrations to lift the spirits of the islanders.

### [Borin Diamondgaze](../npcs/borin-diamondgaze.md)
A former miner who found solace in the worship of Cayden Cailean. He's a skilled brewer and often spends his days tending to the brewery in the House of Storms' territory.
