# House of Storms

A matriarchal dynasty led by [Elara Stormweaver](../npcs/elara-stormweaver.md). Controls the town centre.

**Slang:** The house, the stormers/stormies, gusters.

**Goods:** Consumables — Drugs, Potions (Non-Healing), Clothing & Cloth (including enchanted clothing such as cloaks).

Assets: Potion crafting. Materials acquisition is mostly outsourced but they re-sell at an increased price after processing and refining.

Also the primary patron of Gozreh — Elara's patron deity — giving the House significant religious influence through [The Tidecallers](gozreh.md).

---

## Matriarch: [Elara Stormweaver](../npcs/elara-stormweaver.md)

A cunning and ruthless woman, and a witch. Elara is the undisputed leader of the House of Storms. She has trained many in her household in the art of potion crafting. Her strategic mind and unwavering resolve have solidified the House's position as the owners of the town centre.

## Lieutenants

### [Aella Stormcaller](../npcs/aella-stormcaller.md)
- **Relationship:** Elara's daughter
- **Responsibility:** Oversees the alchemists and the production of drugs.
- **Love:** A passionate affair with Kaelan, the Enforcer.
- **Rivalry:** Competes with her cousin Kaelan for the Matriarch's favour.

### [Kaelan Stormheart](../npcs/kaelan-stormheart.md)
