# The Gilded

The smallest faction but one of the wealthiest. In a land of magic and enchantments, they are kings.

**Slang:** Shinys, posh'n's, rich cunts.

**Goods:** Jewellery, enchantments.

Where [The Three Towers](three-towers.md) are more generalised (research, teach, craft), the Gilded are dedicated craftsmen.
