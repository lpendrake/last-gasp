# The Representatives of the Divine

Unlike other factions, they don't have an area they control, other than their individual shrines and small temples. There is a large effort underway to build a temple big enough to truly honour the gods in the hopes that they will intervene and free the islanders, but it's progressing slowly due to a lack of materials. The places of worship are spread all over town.

Nobody could be bothered saying the full name — they're universally called "the Reps."

**Slang:** The reps, god-botherers.

**Goods:** Consumables, anything religion related, Healing Potions, Divine Spellcasting Services. Medical Services are almost entirely controlled by them — they run a hospital. Only they can resurrect.
