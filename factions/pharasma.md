# Spire Watch (Pharasma)

The temple of [Pharasma](../locations/stormhaven/the-spire.md). Handles burial services, tends the graveyard. Though any religious rep can perform the corpse sanctification ritual, Pharasmans are the default / fallback. Only they can resurrect — and the resources for resurrection are VERY limited. Each other faction has pre-bought the right to 1 resurrection, leaving 1 for the Reps.

Currently sponsors the party's adventuring team.

**Slang:** The reapers, grave watchers. Their temple is The Spire.

### [So-Yun Nightshade](../npcs/so-yun-nightshade.md)
A mysterious and enigmatic figure, deeply devoted to the cycle of life and death. She often communes with the spirits of the dead. Can often be found in secluded areas such as ancient ruins or the outskirts of the city.

### [Morwen Shadowsong](../npcs/morwen-shadowsong.md)
A skilled healer and herbalist. She's often seen tending to the sick and injured, regardless of their faction. Can be found in any of the three main factions' territories, as her healing skills are valued by all.

### [Thrax Blackheart](../npcs/thrax-blackheart.md)
A former member of the House of Storms. He turned to Pharasma after a near-death experience and is now a devout follower, though he still harbours resentment towards his former life. Can often be found in the House of Storms' territory, where he tries to atone for his past sins.
