# Keystone Council (Abadar)

Has advisory priests in every faction. Law enforcement — their paladins run the guards in cooperation with [The Crimson Covenant](asmodeus.md).

[Cato](../party.md) and [Frollo](../party.md) are secretly working to destabilise this faction on behalf of the Asmodeans.

**Slang:** The wardens.

### [Hargrim Stonehelm](../npcs/hargrim-stonehelm.md)
Stern, disciplined, and unwavering in his faith. He believes in strict adherence to laws and order. **Secret:** He has a secret gambling addiction, often sneaking away to the House of Storms' taverns to indulge his vice.

### [Borin Stoneberg](../npcs/borin-stoneberg.md)
A devout follower of Abadar, known for his honesty and integrity. He's often seen mediating disputes between the various factions and is frequently found in the Smokers' territory, given their industrial and structured nature.

### [Sim Silvershield](../npcs/sim-silvershield.md)
A secretive priest with a gambling addiction. He often borrows money from the House of Storms to fuel his habit, putting him in a dangerous position. He can often be found in the House of Storms' territory, frequenting their taverns and gambling dens. Inquisitor of Abadar.
