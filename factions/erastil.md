# Green Watch (Erastil)

Enchanted Leather Goods, Meat, Farmland, Fishing. Meat is a luxury. The Erastil faction established a willingness to fight and kill those that it considered poachers early on. They have a firm grip on hunting, butchering, farming, and fishing.

**Slang:** The hunt, tree huggers.

### [Hank](../npcs/hank.md)
A wise and patient minotaur, he is a master of agriculture and forestry. He believes in hard work and self-sufficiency. He can often be found in the Smokers' territory, given their connection to the land and industry.

### [Fiora Ironfist](../npcs/fiora-ironfist.md)
A skilled blacksmith and weaponsmith. She's often seen working alongside the Smokers, forging tools and weapons. She also advises them on careful mining procedures that won't disrupt local wildlife overmuch.

### [Borin Windrunner](../npcs/borin-windrunner.md)
A former ranger, he now serves Erastil as a protector of the island's forests. He's often seen patrolling the wilderness, ensuring that the balance of nature is maintained. Frequently found in the Free Sails' territory.
