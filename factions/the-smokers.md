# The Smokers

So called because of the smog that the steam powered machines, smelting and forging produce.

A group of goblins, orcs, hobgoblins and other often vilified species who were blamed when the barrier went up. They banded together for protection and have claimed to be innocent of triggering the barrier.

Ironically, they have one of the most free societies on the island because they feel falsely accused and their leader is more smart than charismatic and takes a very hands off approach. This makes them generally very hard to make deals with because he refuses to represent the group as a whole unless they are threatened.

Their leader is a hobgoblin inventor who was hired to help build some industry on the island. As such, he has guided the Smokers to dominate the metal and wood production of the island.

**Slang:** Smokers, smokies, smoggers.

**Goods:** Metal goods — specifically any weapons and armour that are non-magical. They control the only metal mines. Though anyone can go out and chop down a tree, only they have workshops for rapid processing and building materials.

## Leader

### [Krag Ironfist](../npcs/krag-ironfist.md)
- **Class:** Inventor
- A brilliant but gruff hobgoblin, Krag is more interested in tinkering with machines than leading a people. He's a master of steam power and metallurgy, and his inventions have revolutionised the Smokers' industries.
- **Secret:** He's actually an Ancient disguised as a hobgoblin. He's been using the settlement to provide him with resources and feeding them tidbits of his people's tech to stay in charge — that's why he took control of the mines first and then formed the Smokers. He's secretly one of the most powerful people on the island.
