# The Pack

A farming and animal husbandry faction. Heavily influenced by [The Green Watch](erastil.md). Leather and hide are the most common and cheap forms of armour. Meat is a luxury. This faction does well for itself.

**Slang:** Herders, sheep-shaggers, wooly jumpers.

**Goods:** Leather and meat. Heavily influenced by [The Green Watch](erastil.md).
