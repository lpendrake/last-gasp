# Crimson Covenant (Asmodeus)

Has advisory priests in every faction. Law enforcement — their paladins run the guards in cooperation with [the Abadarians](abadar.md).

Secretly directing [Cato](../party.md) to destabilise the [Keystone Council](abadar.md).

**Slang:** Devils, lawyers, tyrants, hellhounds, peacemakers.

### [Thrax Lightwish](../npcs/thrax-lightwish.md)
A cunning and manipulative human, he is a master of intrigue and deception. He believes in order and control.

### [Seraphina Vane](../npcs/seraphina-vane.md)
Chillingly precise and utterly devoted to the letter of the law. She possesses an analytical mind, capable of dissecting any contract or testimony to find its hidden flaws or truths. She is stern, humorless, and rarely shows emotion, but there's a subtle, almost predatory satisfaction she derives from seeing justice (as defined by Asmodeus) strictly applied. She often serves as a lead investigator for contract breaches or civic disobedience, and her infernal heritage is a constant, stark reminder of her faith's power. She views emotional pleas as weakness and chaotic disruptions as anathema.

**Role:** Chief Investigator and Prosecutor for the Asmodean courts, often seen reviewing petitions and training junior guards and legal scholars.

### [Valerius Kael](../npcs/valerius-kael.md)
Outwardly charming, eloquent, and highly charismatic, a master of diplomacy and persuasion. He embodies the manipulative and social aspects of Asmodeus's doctrine, understanding that true power lies in influence and control, not just brute force. He is pragmatic, always looking for opportunities to expand the Church's (and his own) influence within the city's power structures. While perfectly capable of ruthless action, he prefers to achieve his goals through carefully orchestrated agreements and psychological leverage.

**Role:** Chief Administrator of the Temple's civic duties, liaison with the Temple of Abadar, and master of public relations for the Asmodean cause.
