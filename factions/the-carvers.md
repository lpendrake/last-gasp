# The Carvers

The construction and infrastructure faction. Though not adventuring-oriented, the town literally falls apart without them.

**Slang:** Stoners, callused, bloodless.

**Goods:** Wood and Stone, city infrastructure and repair.

### [Rixx Sourbark](../npcs/rixx-sourbark.md)
- **Ancestry:** Orc | **Class:** Ranger | **Industry:** Lumber
- A strong and agile orc, Rixx is a skilled lumberjack and woodsman. He knows the forest like the back of his hand and can fell a tree with a single swing of his axe.

### [Krix Spark](../npcs/krix-spark.md)
- **Ancestry:** Goblin | **Class:** Magus | **Industry:** Engineering and Mechanics
- A brilliant goblin engineer, Krix is always tinkering with new inventions. He's a master of arcane technology.

### [Borka Rockheart](../npcs/borka-rockheart.md)
- **Ancestry:** Hobgoblin | **Class:** Cleric of Shelyn | **Industry:** Construction and Infrastructure
- A devout hobgoblin cleric, Borka is responsible for overseeing construction and infrastructure. A strong believer in community and hard work.
