# The Free Sails

A diverse group of sailors, pirates, merchants, and explorers who found themselves stranded on the island when the magical barrier rose. United by their shared experience and a desire for freedom, they formed a unique community aboard their ships.

They have the biggest alcohol production business. They also provide a large amount of food for the island in the form of fish and extract protection money from anyone who wants to be a fisherman without joining them. Not many choose that option though.

The alchemist Captain Swiftwind also provides a number of drugs and alchemical solutions via a small group of alchemists he has working on the far side of the island, in secret.

**Slang:** Pirates, sailors, the sails.

**Goods:** Food (fish specifically), Alchemical items, cheap drugs (especially alcohol), Elixirs, Mutagens, Poisons, a surprisingly good selection of alcohol.

## The Council of Captains

### [Captain Anya Blackheart](../npcs/anya-blackheart.md)
- **Ancestry:** Human (Varisian)
- **Class:** Rogue (Mastermind)
- **Original Ship:** The Black Pearl
- **Trade:** Piracy
- **First Mate:** [Kael Swiftwind](../npcs/kael-swiftwind.md) (Human)
- A seasoned pirate captain known for her ruthlessness and cunning. She is the de facto leader of the Free Sails, commanding respect through her fearsome reputation and strategic mind.

### [Captain Elias Sunshadow](../npcs/elias-sunshadow.md)
- **Ancestry:** Elf (Undine, water elemental)
- **Class:** Ranger (Outwit)
- **Original Ship:** The Golden Dawn
- **Trade:** Sea Monster Hunter (fantasy Whaler equivalent)
- **First Mate:** [Elara Moonwhisper](../npcs/elara-moonwhisper.md) (Half-Elf, bastard of the captain)
- A noble merchant captain, driven by a sense of justice and a desire to restore order to the island. He seeks to establish a fair and equitable society, often clashing with Anya's more pragmatic approach.

### [Captain Zephyr Swiftwind](../npcs/zephyr-swiftwind.md)
- **Ancestry:** Gnome (Sensate, scent 30ft)
- **Class:** Alchemist (Mutagenist)
- **Original Ship:** The Wind Rider
- **Trade:** Exploration
- **First Mate:** [Torin Windchaser](../npcs/torin-windchaser.md) (Halfling)
- A charismatic and adventurous explorer, always seeking new horizons and opportunities. Skilled navigator and tactician, often leading daring expeditions to the island's unexplored regions.

### [Captain Aella Nightshade](../npcs/aella-nightshade.md)
- **Ancestry:** Elf (Cavern)
- **Class:** Rogue (Arcane Trickster)
- **Original Ship:** The Midnight Phantom
- **Trade:** Merchant (Smuggling)
- **First Mate:** [Krom Sailstalker](../npcs/krom-sailstalker.md) (Cavern Elf)
- A mysterious figure with a shadowy past. Skilled spy and assassin, often operating in the shadows to protect the Free Sails from threats both internal and external.

### [Captain Faran Sunstone](../npcs/faran-sunstone.md)
- **Ancestry:** Dwarf (Death Warden)
- **Class:** Fighter
- **Original Ship:** The Morning Star
- **Trade:** Merchant (Legitimate)
- **First Mate:** [Nyra Starbright](../npcs/nyra-starbright.md) (Half-Elf)
