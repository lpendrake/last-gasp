# The Three Towers

Where [The Gilded](the-gilded.md) are dedicated craftsmen, the Three are more generalised. They research and study, they teach, and yes, they craft.

**Slang:** TTT, the Three.

**Goods:** Magical Trinkets and doodads.

**Tiers:**
- Low tier (Lowest): levels −1 to 2
- Mid tier: levels 3–6
- High tier (Highest): levels 7–10
- The masters are always level 13
