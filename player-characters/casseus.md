# Casseus The Gorgon
Casseus The Gorgon
Ancestry: Minotaur
Class: Demonic Dragon Instinct Barbarian
Weapon of choice: Dorn-Dergar
Worships: Nobody in particular, but appreciates asmodean values of discipline.
Notes: focused on a “commander” flavour via the Marshal Dedication (intimidation focused)

## Spies
Hired spies, just beggars, via the teams “buttler”, Sayon Goro. They can be used to feed the team rumours and hints so long as it’s something a beggar might hear on the street, so no big secrets.

## First Follower
[Sayon Goro](../npcs/sayon-goro.md) was secretly almost immediately recruited by the wizard [Constantine Lightspinner](../npcs/constantine-lightspinner.md) to spy on the team.
