# Kaelan Stormcaller

A Half-Orc notable priest of [Gozreh / The Tidecallers](../factions/gozreh.md). A former pirate who found redemption in the worship of Gozreh. Skilled sailor, often leads the Free Sails in their fishing expeditions.

*(Note: distinct from [Kaelan Stormheart](kaelan-stormheart.md), the House of Storms enforcer.)*
