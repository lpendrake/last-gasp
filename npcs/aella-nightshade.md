# Captain Aella Nightshade

Cavern Elf Rogue (Arcane Trickster) and captain of [The Free Sails](../factions/free-sails.md). Former captain of *The Midnight Phantom*, a smuggling merchant vessel. A mysterious figure with a shadowy past — skilled spy and assassin, operates in the shadows to protect the Free Sails.

First Mate: Krom Sailstalker (Cavern Elf).

*(Note: distinct from [Aella Stormcaller](aella-stormcaller.md) of the House of Storms.)*
