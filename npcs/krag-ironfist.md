# Krag Ironfist

Hobgoblin Inventor and leader of [The Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md). More interested in tinkering with machines than leading. Takes a hands-off approach to governance, making the Smokers very difficult to deal with as a bloc.

Secretly one of the Ancients, disguised as a hobgoblin and using the settlement to provide him with resources, feeding the Smokers tidbits of Ancient technology to maintain his position.
