# Captain Zephyr Swiftwind

A Gnome (Sensate) Alchemist-Mutagenist and captain of the Free Sails. Master alchemist and charismatic explorer. Loaned [Aella Stormcaller](aella-stormcaller.md) the drug-lab ship in exchange for a cut of the drug profits. Also secretly produces drugs and alchemical solutions via a small group of alchemists on the far side of the island.
