# Archmage Constantine Lightspinner

Archmage of the [Lumina Spire](../locations/stormhaven/lumina-spire.md), the highest-level arcane caster in [Stormhaven](../locations/stormhaven.md). An Automaton. Leader of the Arcane tower within [The Three Towers](../factions/three-towers.md).

Secretly recruited [Sayon Goro](sayon-goro.md) to spy on the party almost immediately after they arrived in town.
