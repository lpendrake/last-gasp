# Dara Stormwhisper

Daughter of [Torin Stormfist](torin-stormfist.md) and Lieutenant of [The House of Storms](../factions/house-of-storms.md). Manages the faction's network of informants and spies. Close friends with her cousin Torin (Jr.). Respects her grandmother [Elara Stormweaver](elara-stormweaver.md) for her cunning.
