# Lady So-Yun Nightshade

High Priest of [Pharasma](../factions/pharasma.md) and head of [The Spire Watch](../factions/pharasma.md). A Cavern Elf, mysterious and enigmatic, deeply devoted to the cycle of life and death.

Charged [Vittoria](../party.md) to keep an eye on the party and prevent them causing too much political trouble in town. Her deputy [Morwen Shadowsong](morwen-shadowsong.md) covets her position and considers her too cautious.
