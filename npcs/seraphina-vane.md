# Inquisitor Seraphina Vane

A Female Tiefling and Chief Investigator and Prosecutor for the Asmodean courts, answering to [Thrax Lightwish](thrax-lightwish.md). Chillingly precise, utterly devoted to the letter of the law. Derives quiet satisfaction from seeing Asmodean justice strictly applied.

[Cato](../party.md) has a crush on her. She is one of the [Crimson Covenant](../factions/asmodeus.md) contacts secretly directing him to destabilise the Abadarians.
