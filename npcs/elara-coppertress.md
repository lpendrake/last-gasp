# Elara "Mother Spoons" Coppertress

A Dwarf (Strong-Blooded Heritage) former quartermaster for a mercenary company that went bust. Elected by popular vote as 2nd in command at [The Keep](../locations/the-keep.md). Campaign promise: "A full belly and a clean bedroll for everyone. Order through comfort."

Organises the unglamorous but vital jobs at the keep — latrine digging, water boiling schedules, stretching rations.
