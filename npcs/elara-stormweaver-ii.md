# Elara Stormweaver II

Daughter of [Torin Stormfist](torin-stormfist.md) and Lieutenant of [The House of Storms](../factions/house-of-storms.md). Named after her grandmother [Elara Stormweaver](elara-stormweaver.md). Oversees diplomatic relations and negotiates alliances with other factions. Competes with her cousin [Nyra Stormsight](nyra-stormsight.md) for the Matriarch's favour.
