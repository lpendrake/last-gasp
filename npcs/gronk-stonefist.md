# Gronk Stonefist

An Orc fighter and Mining Industry Leader for [The Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md). Reports to [Krag Ironfist](krag-ironfist.md). Known for a love of explosives and a knack for finding valuable resources.

Tasked the party with dealing with the plane of Earth portal at the quarry, insisting on secrecy to protect the Smokers from political fallout.
