# Captain Elias Sunshadow

Elf (Undine, water elemental) Ranger (Outwit) and captain of [The Free Sails](../factions/free-sails.md). Former captain of *The Golden Dawn*, a sea monster hunter (fantasy whaler equivalent). A noble captain driven by justice and a desire for order, often clashing with Anya Blackheart's more pragmatic approach.

First Mate: Elara Moonwhisper (Half-Elf, his bastard daughter).
