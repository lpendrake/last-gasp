# Morwen Shadowsong

A Cavern Elf healer and priestess of [Pharasma](../factions/pharasma.md). Skilled herbalist known for tending to the sick and injured regardless of faction. Uses her healing to earn favours and manipulate people.

Politically ambitious — wants to take over as leader of the temple of Pharasma, believing [Lady So-Yun](so-yun-nightshade.md) is too cautious. Organised the crew for the party's drug-lab ship and used court connections to facilitate its transfer to the party.
