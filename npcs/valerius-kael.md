# Deacon Valerius Kael

A Human (Cheliaxian) deacon of [Asmodeus / The Crimson Covenant](../factions/asmodeus.md). Outwardly charming, eloquent, highly charismatic — a master of diplomacy and persuasion. Chief Administrator of the temple's civic duties, liaison with the Temple of Abadar, and master of public relations for the Asmodean cause. Achieves goals through carefully orchestrated agreements and psychological leverage rather than brute force.
