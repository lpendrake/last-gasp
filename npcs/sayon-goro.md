# Sayon Goro

A local resident of [Stormhaven](../locations/stormhaven.md) coerced by the party into giving them a tour of the city when they first arrived. Has since become their informal butler and handled the hiring of their beggar spy network.

Secretly recruited by [Constantine Lightspinner](constantine-lightspinner.md) to spy on the party almost immediately after being pressed into service.
