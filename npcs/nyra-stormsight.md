# Nyra Stormsight

Daughter of [Torin Stormfist](torin-stormfist.md) and Lieutenant of [The House of Storms](../factions/house-of-storms.md). Manages the faction's financial affairs and oversees the taverns and gambling dens. Close friends with her cousin [Aella Stormcaller](aella-stormcaller.md). Deputy representative on the City Council.
