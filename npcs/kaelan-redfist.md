# Kaelan Redfist

A Half-Orc High Priest of [Cayden Cailean / The Cup Bearers](../factions/cayden-cailean.md). Boisterous, jovial, known for his love of ale, games, and good company. Believes in living life to the fullest. Frequently found in the taverns of the House of Storms or the Free Sails' floating city.
