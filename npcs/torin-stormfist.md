# Torin Stormfist

Son of [Elara Stormweaver](elara-stormweaver.md) and Lieutenant of [The House of Storms](../factions/house-of-storms.md). Oversees security and intelligence operations. Deeply loyal to his mother. Competes with his cousin [Kaelan Stormheart](kaelan-stormheart.md) for the Enforcer's position.

Father of [Nyra Stormsight](nyra-stormsight.md), [Elara Stormweaver II](elara-stormweaver-ii.md), and [Dara Stormwhisper](dara-stormwhisper.md).
