# Slym Slithertongue

A Kobold Rogue and Trade/Smuggling Industry Leader for [The Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md). Also serves as the Smokers' deputy on the City Council when [Krag Ironfist](krag-ironfist.md) (who never attends) sends a representative. Sly, cunning, mastermind of the Smokers' black market operations.
