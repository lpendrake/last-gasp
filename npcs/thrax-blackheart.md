# Thrax Blackheart

A Human notable priest of [Pharasma / The Spire Watch](../factions/pharasma.md). A former member of [The House of Storms](../factions/house-of-storms.md) who turned to Pharasma after a near-death experience. Devout but harbours resentment toward his former life. Often found in House of Storms territory, trying to atone.

*(Note: distinct from [Thrax Lightwish](thrax-lightwish.md), the Asmodean High Priest.)*
