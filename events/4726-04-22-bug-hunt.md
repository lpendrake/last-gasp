Party tracks the bugs all day, losing the trail twice. They kill a swarm that was chasing loggers and drag the corpses back for [Quinshu](../npcs/quinshu.md) to harvest (6 successes, 2 failures; takes two days).

The next day they find the main nest, prep anti-acid spells, fight the queen, and win. Butchering the queen alone takes 9 hours. They return to the loggers' camp.
