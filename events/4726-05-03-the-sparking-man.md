During the murder investigation, the party encounters a drug addict who combusts in blue electricity in [Abadar's Bazaar](../locations/stormhaven/abadars-bazaar.md). The Abadarian guards redirect the party to their inquisitor (found gambling). He sends them to farms outside town where cows have been similarly combusting.

Investigation finds a device on a pasture channelling static lightning magic into the ground, causing glowing blue flows that the cows are eating. [Hank](../npcs/hank.md) at the temple of Erastil points them to the docks; they track down a dealer who admits his boss is [Aella Stormcaller](../npcs/aella-stormcaller.md), working with [Zephyr Swiftwind](../npcs/zephyr-swiftwind.md) of the [Free Sails](../factions/free-sails.md), who loaned her the ship in exchange for a cut.

*In-game date estimated. Notes state this happened "on the 3rd and ending early on the 4th" of Desnus.*
