A series of ritual murders begins in [Stormhaven](../locations/stormhaven.md). Three murders have occurred by the time the party investigates; each body is arranged to draw a symbol — an inverted occult mark. The pattern is being orchestrated by [The Cult](../factions/the-cult.md), a group under the influence of a conspirator dragon who is sowing chaos. Six murders total are planned.

Party deploys their beggar spies to watch the areas where they predict the next murders will occur. A street seer in a shady alley identifies likely future murder sites.
