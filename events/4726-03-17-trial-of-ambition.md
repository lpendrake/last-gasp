[Cato](../party.md) competes in the Asmodean Trial of Ambition and wins all three tests. The party accuses Acolyte [Valencia](../npcs/acolyte-valencia.md) of stealing gold from the temple — it's unclear if they were right.

Cato negotiates a contract with the [Crimson Covenant](../factions/asmodeus.md): receives a Ring of Devilish Charm and a sample red wine bottle in exchange for 720gp paid over 20 weeks (36gp/week from Pharast 24). The contract comes with hidden devil-clause conditions.
