[Player Characters](../party.md) Cato has a dream next time he rests: 

*As you surrender to rest, the familiar quiet of sleep is replaced by a heavy, suffocating presence—a cold, absolute weight that feels less like a dream and more like a personal audit of your soul. You sense a gaze upon you, sharp and unblinking, as if the Prince of Darkness himself is reviewing the terms of your service.*

*Your mind drifts to the familiar, drafty halls of the Crimson Convent in Stormhaven where you offer your weekly tithes for the Ring of Devilish Charm. In the vision, you stand before the desk of the Infernal Registrar. The massive, soul-bound ledger flips open, its vellum pages smelling of old iron and ozone as they flip rapidly to a specific page.*

*Your eyes find the line dedicated to your name. You see the ink clearly: the meticulously recorded "Amount Due" and the tally of the gold you still owe for the ring. As you stare at the numbers, the ink begins to smoke. Suddenly, a flare of dark red, black-tinged hellfire erupts from the page. The flames dance specifically over that debt entry, devouring the script in a silent, searing decree of the Archfiend's attention.*

*The heat is not confined to the dream. On your finger, the ring pulses with a branding intensity. It grows uncomfortably hot, the metal seemingly tightening. You feel the permanent, heavy weight of blackened steel links locking into place around your finger.*
