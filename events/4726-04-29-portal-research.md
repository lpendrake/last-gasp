[Gronk Stonefist](../npcs/gronk-stonefist.md) asks the party to deal with the portal quietly — if other factions find out, they'll use it against the Smokers. Party splits up:

- [Belwar](../party.md) spends the day researching portals (7 hours).
- [Vittoria](../party.md) crafts [Casseus](../party.md)'s new weapon (success).
- [Casseus](../party.md) makes friends with the common folk — spends a fortune on booze and maxes out the easy relationship tier with the Folk of Stormhaven.
- [Cato](../party.md) and [Frollo](../party.md) consult the towers. [Cassia](../npcs/cassia.md) (Witch Adept, half-orc) at [The Whispering Claw](../locations/stormhaven/the-whispering-claw.md) agrees to create a containment vessel; asks them to return tomorrow. [Anya Starweaver](../npcs/anya-starweaver.md) at [The Lumina Spire](../locations/stormhaven/lumina-spire.md) wants 300gp or to be taken to the site directly.

[Gorbeka](../npcs/gorbeka.md) of the [Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md) is also consulted the next session — she suggests planting elemental lightning rods around the portal site. An Asmodean bureaucratic inspector confronts the party at the quarry; they humiliate him while keeping the portal secret from him. **+1 Smokers, −1 Asmodeans.**
