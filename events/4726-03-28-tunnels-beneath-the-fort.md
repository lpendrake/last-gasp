The party clears the fort interior and fights the boss goblin. They then delve into the tunnels below, find and kill the creature dominating the goblins and orcs, and get ambushed by two huge lizards — one of which eats a horse. [Quinshu](../npcs/quinshu.md) attempts to harvest the slain lizard but fails. Deeper exploration leads to the discovery of an ancient lab.
