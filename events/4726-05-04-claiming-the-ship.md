After resting, the party consults their sponsor and chooses to take the ship. They make a deal with the Abadarian inquisitor — no pay, but he pulls strings to get the ship assigned to the [Pharasma](../factions/pharasma.md)-sponsored team as a tool for investigating the barrier.

[Morwen Shadowsong](../npcs/morwen-shadowsong.md) organises the crew (who are indebted to her for saving their lives) and asks the court to donate the ship to the cause. New captain: [Abner Marsh](../npcs/abner-marsh.md). First mate: Zok (half-orc). Crew: Skak and Drix Claybarb (Gnoll).

Party then sails to [the quarry mine](../locations/the-quarry.md) to close the portal. They encounter rogue druids trying to stop anyone brute-forcing the closure — the party defeats them and makes a deal: the druids help by appeasing the spirits so fewer elementals spawn during the ritual.
