Party visits the first [quarry](../locations/the-quarry.md). They agree to deal with a pissed-off spirit after the [Smokers](../factions/the-smokers.md) blow up a hill for mining access. During the fight, a tentacled creature appears and tears open a rift to the plane of Earth. An earth elemental stabilises the portal at the cost of its own life, out of spite. [Casseus](../party.md) tries to smash the tear with his weapon — the weapon breaks.

Party returns to town, carrying a message to the Smokers about the portal. **DING — Level 8.**

[Gronk Stonefist](../npcs/gronk-stonefist.md) asks them to return tomorrow; he may need help.
